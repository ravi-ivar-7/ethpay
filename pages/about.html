Our Mission

ethpay was created to simplify BAT (Basic Attention Token) payments within the Brave ecosystem. We believe that cryptocurrency payments should be as easy as sharing a link.

Why ethpay?

Traditional cryptocurrency payments require copying long addresses, which is error-prone and inconvenient. ethpay solves this by generating shareable payment links with QR codes, making BAT transactions as simple as clicking a link.

Built for Brave

As part of the Brave Website-Building Challenge, ethpay showcases the power of the Brave ecosystem by integrating with Brave Wallet, utilizing BAT tokens, and providing a seamless user experience for the Brave community.

Technology

ethpay is built with pure HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript - no frameworks required. This ensures fast loading times, IPFS compatibility, and a lightweight footprint. We integrate with CoinGecko for live BAT statistics and Etherscan for blockchain data.
